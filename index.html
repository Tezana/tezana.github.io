How I Document My Labs

Every lab in this portfolio is documented to show not just what I built, but how I thought through the process. Each entry includes the objective, tools used, steps completed, issues encountered, troubleshooting actions, screenshots, lessons learned, and next steps.

This approach prepares me to communicate technical work clearly — in tickets, runbooks, post-mortems, and interviews.

  1. Step 01Plan the lab objective and scope
  2. Step 02Build the environment or scenario
  3. Step 03Document each step as it happens
  4. Step 04Troubleshoot issues and record root cause
  5. Step 05Capture screenshots and command output
  6. Step 06Write lessons learned and next steps
  7. Step 07Publish to GitHub Pages with a dedicated lab page
