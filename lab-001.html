LAB-001

PC Hardware
Identification Lab

A hands-on break and fix lab documenting the identification, removal, inspection, and reinstallation of major PC components. Each step was photographed and documented to build hardware recognition and troubleshooting skills aligned with CompTIA A+ objectives.

Status In Progress
Category CompTIA A+ / Hardware
Difficulty Beginner
Date 2026
// Overview

Lab Details

Objective
  • Identify all major internal PC components
  • Document each component's purpose and function
  • Practice safe removal and reinstallation
  • Build hardware troubleshooting skills
  • Document findings with photos and notes
Tools Used
  • Desktop PC (break and fix unit)
  • Phillips head screwdriver
  • Anti-static wrist strap
  • Thermal paste
  • Compressed air
  • CompTIA A+ study objectives
  • Documentation template
Skills Demonstrated
  • Hardware component recognition
  • Safe PC disassembly procedures
  • ESD awareness and prevention
  • Technical documentation
  • Troubleshooting methodology
  • Attention to detail
